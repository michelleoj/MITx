Cafeteria Knapsack Problem

You are at a cafeteria and you're trying to figure out what is the maximum amount of calories you can consume with a budget of only $20! What strategy should you use to maximize your caloric intake?

Here are some strategies you can consider. Considering the food items remaining on the shelf will fit in your stomach, you can choose the food based on:

  1. "Max Value" -- choose the item with the greatest amount of calories
  2. "Min Weight" -- choose the item with the lowest price
  3. "Max Value/Weight" -- choose the item with largest calories-to-price ratio
